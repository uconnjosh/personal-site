In this business, constant learning is a given, but the most valuable lesson I've learned so far is to embrace simplicity. I enjoy working with technologies that will yield the end result in a timely manner, and at a low cost. Doing this involves choosing a tech stack that's just right for your project, nothing more and nothing less.
