About

Hi, I'm Josh! I grew up in a small town in Connecticut. It was in that same town that I also attended the University of Connecticut for college. After graduating in 2013, I decided that I wanted two things: A change of scenery, and to become a software developer. So I bought a one-way ticket to beautiful Portland, Oregon where I attended a coding bootcamp called Epicodus. It was a great experience that lead to a job at FocusVision, which consists of a myriad of tools and platforms for market research data collection.

I worked at FocusVision for three years. During my time there, I was a jack of all trades type. I did a lot of API development in Ruby/Rails (with a small amount of C#) as well as front-end development using ember. I learned that while server side development has its own satisfactions, I'm more drawn to full-stack and front-end work because I enjoy the visual satisfaction of making an idea come to life in the browser.

I'm now happily working in a front-end heavy role at BOND, a digital agency in Hollywood. I've had the opportunity to work on an array of visually dazzling, high traffic projects. In addition to becoming a front-end expert, the fast paced, client-oriented nature of life at BOND has also helped me to become better at prioritizing tasks and communicating needs to project stakeholders.
